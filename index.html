FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Stratvex?+
Getting started is simple. Create your free account, complete KYC verification, deposit funds and choose a plan. The entire process takes under 10 minutes.
When can I withdraw my profits?+
You can request a profit withdrawal at any time once your investment is active. There is no lock-in period for profits — simply request via your dashboard and our team processes it promptly.
What is the minimum amount I can invest?+
You can start with as little as $100 through our entry-level plan. As your capital grows, you can upgrade to higher tiers offering greater returns — all the way up to the VIP Plan with unlimited amounts.
Are my funds safe on Stratvex?+
Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption, cold storage for digital assets and maintain reserves of 150% of total deposits. All accounts are protected by multi-step verification including transaction PINs and withdrawal codes.
What currencies are supported?+
We support 18 currencies including USD, GBP, EUR, CAD, AUD, CHF, JPY, AED, SGD, SAR, ZAR, GHS, KES, INR, BRL, MXN, NZD and more. You select your currency during registration.
Can I invest in multiple plans simultaneously?+
Yes, you can hold multiple active investments across different plans and asset types at the same time, allowing you to diversify and maximise returns across multiple markets.
How long does KYC verification take?+
KYC verification is typically completed within 24-48 hours. You will receive a notification as soon as your documents are reviewed and approved by our compliance team.
Does Stratvex offer loans and savings plans?+
Yes. In addition to investment plans, Stratvex offers personal, business, automobile, mortgage and health finance loans. We also offer high-yield savings plans with interest rates up to 12% over 30 to 90 day periods.
